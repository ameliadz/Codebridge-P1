Outlander

Created by Diana Gabaldon

The Books

Main series:

  1. Outlander* (1991)
  2. Dragonfly in Amber (1992)
  3. Voyager (1993)
  4. Drums of Autumn (1996)
  5. The Fiery Cross (2001)
  6. A Breath of Snow and Ashes (2005)
  7. An Echo in the Bone (2009)
  8. Written in My Own Heart's Blood (2014)
  9. Go Tell the Bees That I Am Gone (forthcoming)
    10. *Released as Cross Stitch in the U.K., Australia, and New Zealand

Related Books:

  • The Exile: An Outlander Graphic Novel (2010)
    • A graphic novel of Jamie's experience of the first third of the first Outlander book.
  • The Outlandish Companion Vols. One and Two (2015)
    • A companion book of synopses, character guides, and other notes and information.
  • Seven Stones to Stand or Fall (2017)
    • A collection of Outlander novellas and short stories.
  • The Lord John series
    • A spin-off series of historical mystery novels and novellas centered on a secondary character in the main Outlander series.
